Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers for students, parents and serious singing learners.

Do beginners join your singing classes?

Yes. Beginners are welcome, and the foundation plan is designed to build correct basics in sur, breathing and confidence.

Is the coach experienced?

Yes. The studio is guided by a coach with over 15 years of experience helping singers improve technique and performance.

Do you help with stage fear?

Yes. Confidence building, expression training and performance preparation are part of the coaching process.

What is the best way to contact the studio?

The fastest option is WhatsApp on 9675490266.

Are there different course plans?

Yes. There are beginner, growth and mentorship plans, plus tailored options for kids and specific goals.

Can you help if I already sing but feel stuck?

Yes. Many students join because they practice regularly but still struggle with tuning, stamina, expression or consistency.
